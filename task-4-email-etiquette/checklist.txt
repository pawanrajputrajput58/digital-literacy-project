# Social Media Do's and Don'ts for College Students

## ✅ Do's

1. Think before posting — ensure your content reflects your values and future goals.
2. Maintain a professional profile — use a clear profile picture and appropriate bio.
3. Verify information before sharing — avoid spreading misinformation.
4. Use privacy settings wisely — control who can see your personal content.
5. Engage respectfully — communicate politely in comments and discussions.
6. Build a positive digital presence — share achievements, projects, and skills.

## ❌ Don'ts

1. Do not post offensive or inappropriate content — it can harm your reputation.
2. Avoid sharing personal or sensitive information (address, phone number, etc.).
3. Do not engage in online arguments or toxic discussions.
4. Avoid posting impulsively when emotional — it can lead to regret.
5. Do not plagiarize or repost content without giving credit.
6. Avoid oversharing — maintain a balance between personal and public life.
